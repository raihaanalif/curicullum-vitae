Project Description:

Make an application design to help cake shop owner to sell their cake to customer with online method

Project Role:

My role in this project as UI UX Designer

Project Overview

This project is go along with design thinking pattern and started with emphatize. This emphatize is define with Binar Academy Challenge that we must create an application design that can help a cake shop owner to sell their cake to customer with online method. The first thing that i did is to benchmarking with 2 biggest online food order like Grab and Gojek and compare the advantages and disadvantages of each of them. But the point of advantages and disadvantages is not by my asumption only, i took that point from interview with my friend which is the customer of Grab and Gojek. The result of interview is displayed with user journey map and with bahasa language:

After i took an interview with two of my friends, I make a map and fill with some point that i took from interview with my friend. The point that i took from interview. After that activity, i make a wireframe that can help me to make a prototype of the application. You can see my full define and ideation phase on this link.

After that phase, i started to make a design prototype from design wireframe that i made in the previous phase. I also made a design system to help me finish this prototype.

Project Prototype

Project Result

I took a usabillity testing to get review and feedback from customer to the next design step. First step that i do is make a scenario to test the design and make design for usabillity testing. After that, i looked for a customer or client that relate with my user persona and finally this is the result from the usabiliity testing from Post Test Question.
